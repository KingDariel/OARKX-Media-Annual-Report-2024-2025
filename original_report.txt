OARKX MEDIA

Annual Report (2024 – 2025)


---

1. Executive Summary

The 2024–2025 fiscal year marked a transformative phase for OARKX Media as it evolved from a traditional digital marketing agency into a multi-division media, entertainment, and technology ecosystem. Headquartered in Ghana, OARKX Media expanded its reach both locally and internationally, delivering measurable value across marketing, content creation, and tech education.

Through a strong focus on innovation, audience engagement, and data-driven strategies, the company achieved consistent growth while building platforms that empower businesses, creators, and aspiring tech professionals.


---

2. Company Overview

OARKX Media is a Ghana-based digital media and technology company with a diversified structure designed to integrate marketing, entertainment, and education.

Core Divisions

OARKX Media (Marketing Division)

Social Media Marketing

SEO & Content Strategy

Paid Advertising (Meta & Google Ads)

Lead Generation & Conversion Optimization


OARKX Music Studio
A creative hub for music production, artist development, and audio engineering, supporting emerging and professional talents.

OARKX Interactive Platform
A gamified engagement system where audiences participate in quizzes, competitions, and reward-based activities to win prizes and recognition.

OARKX Podcast Studio
A professional environment for podcast recording, storytelling, interviews, and industry discussions.

OARKX Tech Studio
An educational division focused on training aspiring tech professionals in coding, programming, and digital skills through practical, hands-on learning.

OARKX Build (Upcoming)
Focuses on building luxury houses and mansions with a digital face for global stakeholders, offering high-end real estate and digital construction solutions.

OARKX NEWS
Where latest and authentic news is shared and publicly and among subscribers on the OARKX app.
